Animals

Animals are a diverse group of living organisms that inhabit various ecosystems across the globe, ranging from microscopic organisms to massive creatures like whales and elephants. They display a wide range of behaviors, adaptations, and physical characteristics that allow them to survive and thrive in their respective environments. From providing companionship as pets to serving vital roles in ecological systems, animals play an integral part in the interconnected web of life on Earth.
